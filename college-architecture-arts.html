Project Overview

As part of Vision 2030, the College of Architecture and Arts in Riyadh serves as a specialized center for creative education, bridging the gap between heritage and modernity. The college aims to nurture a new generation of innovators capable of competing globally while supporting sustainable development and strengthening the Kingdom's position as a hub for design and creativity.

Design Philosophy: "Infinity"

Inspired by the concept of "Infinity," the design philosophy reflects a comprehensive vision of the interpenetration between man, time, space, and nature. It envisions architecture as a continuous loop—a seamless, organic flow with no beginning or end. This dynamic approach allows the building to evolve and adapt, symbolizing the endless potential for human creativity and the infinite perfection of nature.
